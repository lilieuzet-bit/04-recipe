The perfect scrambled eggs

This scrambled eggs recipe will take you a bit longer than just throwing your eggs in a pan on high heat, but I promise it'll be worth it.

What ingredients do I need?

For one serving:

  • 3 eggs
  • 25g butter
  • 10cl heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk .
  2. Add the heavy cream, salt and pepper and whisk again.
  3. Add the unmelted butter to a small pot (NOT A PAN!) with your eggs before turning the heat on.
  4. Turn the heat on low and stir slowly for 5 to 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the eggs from the heat before they look fully done.
  6. Season with salt and pepper again and serve immediately.

For even more deliciousness...

You can put your eggs on toasted sourdough with some cream cheese and sriracha sauce or chili flakes. It's the perfect scrambled eggs for the perfect breakfast!
